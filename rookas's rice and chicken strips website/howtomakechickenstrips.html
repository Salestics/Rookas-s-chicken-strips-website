How to Make Chicken Strips?

This is how to make chicken strips. First, you need to get some chicken breasts and cut them into strips. Then, you can season the chicken with your favorite spices. After that, you can either bake or fry the chicken strips until they are cooked through and golden brown. Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken strips!

Sauces

There are many sauces that can be eaten with chicken strips! My personal favorite is the "Mayochup sauce".

Mayochup sauce is a mixture of mayonnaise and ketchup.

However, there are many other sauces to dip with! Some include

Classic & Creamy

  • Ranch: The top choice for creamy, herby flavor.
  • Honey Mustard: A perfect blend of sweet and tangy.
  • Blue Cheese: Tangy and pungent, great with spicy chicken.
  • Garlic Aioli/Parmesan: A savory, rich mayo-based option.
  • Alabama White Sauce: A tangy, mayonnaise-based BBQ sauce.

Sweet & Tangy

  • BBQ Sauce: Ranging from sweet Kansas City style to tangy Carolina vinegar.
  • Sweet & Sour: A classic, bright dipping option.
  • Sweet Thai Chili: Mildly spicy and sweet.
  • Honey/Hot Honey: Pure sweetness or spiked with heat.

Spicy & Savory

  • Buffalo Sauce: Classic tangy hot sauce mixed with butter.
  • Sriracha Mayo: Creamy heat.
  • Bang Bang Sauce: A sweet and spicy mayonnaise sauce.
  • Chipotle Ranch/Mayo: Smoky and spicy.
  • Gochojang Ranch: A fermented Korean chili twist.

Unique & Specialty

  • Chick-fil-A Sauce: A smoky, tangy honey mustard blend.
  • Comeback/Raising Cane's Sauce: A tangy blend of mayo, ketchup, and spices.
  • Gravy: Especially popular for fried chicken in Southern cuisine.
