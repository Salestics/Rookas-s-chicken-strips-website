"Rice is a cereal grain and in its domesticated form is the staple food of over half of the world's population, particularly in Asia and Africa." (wikipedia) Rice originates from the grass species Oryza sativa (Asian rice) and Oryza glaberrima (African rice). It provides a major source of calories, carbohydrates, and some protein. Over half of the population of the world consumes rice in some capacity, with China, Indonesia, and India being countries with the largest consumer base.
